Company story

Comfort, adventure, and authenticity can belong in the same journey.

Moor's Wild Safaris is a Zambia-based safari company creating tailor-made travel across Zambia and Southern Africa. The company specializes in mobile safaris, lodge safaris, photographic itineraries, cultural tours, hosted trips, and private transfers.

The brand is designed for travelers who want the wilderness without uncertainty. That means honest planning, local insight, suitable vehicles, clear transfer support, and routes that respect the season as much as the guest.

The visual identity points to a warm and rugged premium position: acacia silhouettes, elephants, a vehicle, canvas camp, and sunset light. The website carries that same promise in a cleaner editorial form.

Mission

Values

What guides the work.

Luxury here is not about excess. It is about timing, knowledge, comfort, honesty, and the confidence of being looked after.

Regional respect

Routes and guest expectations are shaped by real knowledge of place, season, people, and wildlife behavior.

Operational calm

Transport, timing, lodge coordination, and contingency planning are treated as part of the guest experience.

Personal fit

Every trip starts with the guest: pace, comfort, photographic interest, culture, budget range, and travel style.

Guiding philosophy

The best safari is designed around attention.

Attention to seasons. Attention to light. Attention to road time, border crossings, arrival fatigue, and the difference between a good sighting and a rushed one. Moor's Wild Safaris treats those details as the heart of the craft.
