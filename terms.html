Booking Terms

This page is a launch-ready structure for booking terms. Final legal wording, cancellation rules, and payment schedules should be approved by the business.

Planning clarity

Terms should reduce uncertainty.

Safari travel depends on seasonal access, partner availability, park rules, border requirements, and supplier terms. The final terms should make those dependencies clear.

Quotes and availability

Itinerary quotes should remain subject to lodge, camp, transfer, and activity availability until confirmed.

Payments

Deposits, final payment timing, currency, and accepted payment methods should be listed before launch.

Changes and cancellation

Supplier terms, park fees, cancellation windows, and refund conditions should be confirmed in writing.

Travel documents

Guests remain responsible for passports, visas, health requirements, and travel insurance.

Safety and conduct

Guests should follow guide instructions, lodge rules, park regulations, and vehicle safety guidance.

Force majeure

Weather, access changes, border conditions, wildlife movement, and supplier disruptions may require route adjustments.

Questions

Ask before you commit.

The inquiry flow is designed to clarify route, scope, transfer details, and booking expectations before confirmation.
