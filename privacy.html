Privacy Policy

This draft should be reviewed by the business before launch and adjusted to match the final form handling and analytics setup.

Information collected

Inquiry details are used to respond to safari requests.

Moor's Wild Safaris may collect contact details, travel dates, group size, safari interests, and messages submitted through the inquiry form or email links.

This information is used to respond to requests, prepare route ideas, coordinate travel services, and communicate about safari planning.

The site does not include analytics or advertising scripts in this build. If analytics are added later, this page should be updated before launch.

Retention
Keep inquiry data only as long as needed for planning, business records, and legal obligations.
Sharing
Share details only with travel partners when needed to prepare or operate the requested journey.
