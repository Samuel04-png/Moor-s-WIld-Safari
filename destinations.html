Seasonal thinking

Timing changes the experience.

Wildlife, roads, rivers, waterfall flow, park access, weather, beach conditions, and border logistics all change through the year, so destination planning starts with the travel window.

SeasonTypical feelPlanning note
Dry seasonConcentrated wildlife, clearer movement, classic game-drive rhythm, and strong safari conditions.Book key camps, lodges, and cross-border routes early.
Shoulder monthsGood value potential, changing landscapes, varied weather, and flexible combinations.Useful for travelers mixing safari, beach, hiking, and photography.
Green seasonLush scenery, dramatic skies, birding, waterfalls, and a different photographic mood.Route choices depend on access, lodge operations, and activity conditions.
