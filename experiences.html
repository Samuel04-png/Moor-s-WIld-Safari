Travel styles

Experiences shaped around how you travel.

Choose one focus or combine several into a single route: private safaris, luxury lodges, family holidays, beaches, hiking, adventure, photography, culture, and transfers.

Experience taxonomy

One itinerary can hold many modes.

The structure is flexible by design. A guest can land in Lusaka or Livingstone, transfer into a lodge, continue into a mobile camp, add a waterfall hike or cultural day, and finish with a beach or desert extension.

Private and camping safaris

Sleep close to the wild, with the route kept flexible.

Private safaris and camping safaris suit guests who want a more immersive pace, flexible camp atmosphere, and strong access to remote landscapes without giving up thoughtful support.
